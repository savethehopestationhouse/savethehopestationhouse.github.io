Save the Hope Station House

The Hope Station House, built in 1916, is an essential and integral part of the history of the Town of Hope, BC. However, our community is now at risk of losing this important part of our heritage. The District of Hope Council passed a decision to destroy this century-old building in an agreement with the Ministry of Transportation and Infrastructure.

While time is running out, it is still not too late to stand up together and raise our voices to save our history!!

Please read on to learn what you can do.
